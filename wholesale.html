Frequently Asked Questions

What are your minimum order requirements?

Our standard minimum order for wholesale is 1 kg of coffee per order. However, we're flexible and can discuss options based on your specific business needs.

How often do you deliver?

We offer flexible delivery options, including weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly schedules. We'll work with you to establish a delivery cadence that ensures you always have fresh coffee without excess inventory.

Do you offer private labeling?

Yes, we offer private labeling options for businesses looking to sell coffee under their own brand. This service includes custom packaging design and labeling. Minimum order quantities apply for this service.

What training do you provide?

We offer comprehensive training programs for your staff, including basic barista skills, latte art, equipment maintenance, and coffee knowledge. Training can be conducted at your location or at our training facility.

Can you help with equipment selection and setup?

Absolutely! We provide consultation on equipment selection based on your space, volume, and budget. We can also assist with installation and setup to ensure optimal performance.

Do you offer custom blends?

Yes, we can create custom blends tailored to your taste preferences and business needs. Our roasting team will work with you to develop a unique profile that you can call your own.
