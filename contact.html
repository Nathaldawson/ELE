Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can click on this number to view the status of your delivery. You can also log into your account on our website to check your order status.

What is your return policy?

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 30 days. Coffee products must be unopened and in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How should I store my coffee?

For optimal freshness, store coffee in an airtight container it comes in or a glass jar. Keep it in a cool, dark place. Avoid refrigerating as coffee can absorb odors. Whole beans stay fresh longer than ground coffee, so we recommend grinding just before brewing.

Do you offer coffee subscriptions?

Yes! We offer flexible subscription options so you never run out of your favorite coffee. Choose your preferred coffee, delivery frequency, and quantity. You can easily pause, skip, or cancel at any time.
