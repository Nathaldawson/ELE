Our Story

At Ele Coffee Company, we're not just in the business of coffee—we're in the business of creating a sustainable, ethically charged revolution. Born from our passion on a humble 2-acre farm in Thakeri, Coorg, our organic coffee beans are the result of innovative bootstrapping and a deep-rooted commitment to quality. Our journey started with a modest harvest (yes, we once maxed out at 350kgs!), but our ambition grew faster than our coffee plants. Every packet of our premium coffee—whether a perfectly portioned 50gms or a robust 150gms blend—represents our promise: no shortcuts, no exploited labor. We pay our team fairly, because great coffee is brewed with respect and integrity. But that's not all. With every purchase, we plant a tree and reinvest 15% of our revenue back into the local farming community. It's our way of saying, "Thanks for choosing a better cup," while nurturing a greener, more vibrant future. In short, ele Coffee Company isn't just a brand; it's a bold, bean-powered movement disrupting the daily grind. Cheers to ethical innovation, one delicious sip at a time!
